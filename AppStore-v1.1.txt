4.  DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FjordPulse turns the Norwegian government's BarentsWatch fish-health data into a
clean, mobile-friendly view of the country's entire salmon aquaculture industry
— every active farm, every reporting week, on one map.

WHAT YOU SEE

• A live map of all active Norwegian salmon farms along the coast, color coded
  by their current compliance status (healthy / watch / at risk).
• Per-farm detail: adult female sea lice as a percentage of the 0.5/fish
  regulatory limit, mobile and stationary lice categories, sea temperature,
  licensed capacity, environmental ("green") license status, land-based RAS
  facilities, and the official production area (PO1–PO13).
• NEW: live satellite imagery of each farm's real location, right on its
  profile — double-tap it to jump straight to the farm on the map.
• Twelve-week trend charts for lice and water temperature, with Norway's
  regulatory threshold drawn on the chart so you can see at a glance whether a
  farm is trending toward or away from non-compliance.
• Active treatment timeline: thermal delousing, freshwater bath, in-feed
  treatments, mechanical removal, cleaner-fish deployments.
• ILA and PD disease outbreak flags, PD surveillance/protection zone labels,
  and fish escape events.
• A national snapshot in the Insights tab: industry-wide lice averages,
  outbreak counts, water-condition ranges, and municipality hotspots.

NEW IN 1.1 — FAVORITES THAT FOLLOW YOU

• Tap the star on any farm to add it to your favorites, then filter the Farms
  list to just those. Favorite from the list, the farm profile, or the map.
• Save a search — your text plus the status filter and sort — and reapply it
  with a tap.
• Sign in with Apple or Google (optional) to sync your favorites and saved
  searches across every device. Signed out, they still work on this device and
  merge into your account the moment you sign in.

WHO IT'S FOR

• Aquaculture professionals, fish-health technicians and farm operators who
  want a quick mobile read on conditions across the industry.
• Investors and analysts tracking salmon producers (Mowi, SalMar, Lerøy,
  Bakkafrost and others) where lice and disease outbreaks move share prices.
• Environmental researchers, journalists, and wild-salmon advocates following
  the impact of farming on Norwegian fjords.
• Coastal residents, fjord-tourism operators and sport anglers curious about
  what is happening at farms in their region.
• Curious individuals.

PRIVACY

FjordPulse works fully without an account. Signing in with Apple or Google is
optional and only enables syncing your favorite farms and saved searches across
your devices; to do that we store your account email, name, profile picture, and
a user identifier, and nothing more. Farm data comes from BarentsWatch via a
small proxy server we
operate, with Apple App Attest verifying that requests come from a genuine
install. No personal browsing or location history is collected.
[ADS] FjordPulse shows ads through Google AdMob, which collects device and usage
data to deliver them; you can limit personalized ads in iOS Settings.
Read the full Privacy Policy at https://leetsyplakov.github.io/FjordPulse-policies/.

LANGUAGES

The app ships in Norwegian (default — matches the source data) and English.
The English mode translates Norwegian place names via Google Translate and
caches each name on your device so the same name is fetched at most once.

DATA SOURCE

All farm data is from BarentsWatch, the Norwegian government's official marine
open-data service, refreshed weekly when farms file their reports. FjordPulse is
an independent app and is not affiliated with BarentsWatch, the Mattilsynet
(Norwegian Food Safety Authority) or any salmon producer.

INFORMATIONAL USE ONLY

The data in FjordPulse is provided for informational purposes only. It is not
financial, regulatory, or operational advice. Always consult the primary source
and qualified professionals before acting on aquaculture data.

12. APP REVIEW — DEMO INFO  ***UPDATED FOR 1.1***
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sign-in Required = NO. The app is fully usable without an account; sign-in only
syncs favorites and saved searches.

Notes for Apple's reviewer:

  FjordPulse is a read-only visualization of Norwegian government open data from
  BarentsWatch (https://www.barentswatch.no). Network calls go to a small proxy
  at api.pythonenthusiast.com, authenticated via Apple App Attest on real
  devices.

  Accounts are OPTIONAL. We offer Sign in with Apple alongside Sign in with
  Google (Guideline 4.8 compliant). To test sign-in, the reviewer can use their
  own Apple ID (Sign in with Apple) — no demo account is needed; or skip sign-in
  entirely and use favorites/searches locally. Signing in only enables
  cross-device sync of the user's favorite farms and saved searches; we store
  only the account email, name, profile picture, and a user identifier.

  ACCOUNT DELETION (Guideline 5.1.1(v)): signed-in users can permanently delete
  their account and all server data in-app at Settings → Account → Delete
  Account. This wipes the user record, favorites, saved searches, and sessions.

  [ADS] The app displays Google AdMob banner and interstitial ads. There are no
  in-app purchases and no user-generated content.

  Source: https://github.com/leetsyplakov/FjordPulse
  Privacy: https://leetsyplakov.github.io/FjordPulse-policies/privacy.html
