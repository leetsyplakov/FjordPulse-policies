Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-28 · Effective 2026-05-28

This Privacy Policy describes how the FjordPulse iOS app ("FjordPulse", "the app", "we") handles information when you use it. FjordPulse is an independent personal project that visualizes public Norwegian salmon-aquaculture data from BarentsWatch.

What we do not collect

FjordPulse does not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, postal address, or any other contact info.
  • Your precise or coarse location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, health, or motion data.
  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), unique device identifier (IDFV), or fingerprint.
  • Browsing, search, or interaction analytics. The app contains no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no advertising SDK, and no marketing or attribution SDK.

What is stored on your device

FjordPulse stores the following on-device only, via Apple's standard UserDefaults:

  • UI preferences: language, map style, hide-inactive toggle, production-area overlay toggle.
  • A non-personal App Attest key identifier (see below).
  • A local cache of Norwegian-to-English place-name translations (only if you opt into English mode).

This data is removed when you delete the app.

Data sent to our server

FjordPulse fetches farm data from a proxy server we operate at api.pythonenthusiast.com, hosted on DigitalOcean in Frankfurt, Germany. The proxy in turn fetches data from BarentsWatch's public API. The only data the app sends to our server is:

  • The IDs of farms and ISO weeks you are viewing.
  • A static API key embedded in the app.
  • On real iPhones/iPads, an Apple App Attest assertion — a per-device cryptographic proof that you are using a genuine FjordPulse install. The proof contains no personal information.

We do not log IP addresses or assemble any user profile. We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose anything to advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.

App Attest

FjordPulse uses Apple's App Attest framework. On first launch the app generates a key pair inside your device's Secure Enclave; the private key never leaves the device. The public key identifier is sent to our server so we can verify that subsequent requests come from a genuine, unmodified copy of FjordPulse. This identifier is not linked to your Apple ID, your name, or any other personal information, and cannot be used to track you across apps or websites.

Google Translate (opt-in)

FjordPulse defaults to displaying the data in its original Norwegian. If you switch the Language setting to "English", the app sends Norwegian place names (locality, municipality) — not user data — to the unauthenticated public Google Translate endpoint, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Each translation is cached locally so the same name is fetched at most once. Switching back to Norwegian stops all calls to Google Translate.

BarentsWatch

The underlying data is published by BarentsWatch, a Norwegian government service for marine open data. We are not affiliated with BarentsWatch. Their privacy practices apply to the upstream data and are described on their site.

Children

FjordPulse is a general-audience information app. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Your rights

Because FjordPulse does not collect personally identifying information, there is no personal record to access, correct, or delete. To remove the on-device cache and preferences, simply uninstall the app via iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → FjordPulse → Delete App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
